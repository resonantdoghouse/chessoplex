Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Chessoplex ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Account data — If you choose to sign in, we collect your email address and profile information provided by your OAuth provider (e.g. Google).
  • Game data — Move histories (PGN), game results, difficulty settings, and session durations are saved to provide your personal history and stats.
  • Study progress — Which chess openings you have practiced and completion status.
  • Anonymous analytics — Aggregate session data (device type, platform, duration) to understand usage patterns. No personally identifying information is included in anonymous sessions.

How We Use Your Information

  • To save and display your game history and study progress across devices.
  • To display global statistics (total players, total games played).
  • To improve the app based on aggregate usage patterns.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a hosted PostgreSQL service. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Aggregated, anonymized statistics may be displayed publicly within the app (e.g. total global games played).

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at jim.webdesign@gmail.com.

Guest / Anonymous Play

You may play Chessoplex without creating an account. In this case, only an anonymous session identifier (stored locally on your device) is used to count unique players — no personal data is collected.
